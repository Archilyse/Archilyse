Controls and Keyboard Shortcuts
Instructions
  • Press the button "Save" to save the changes and finish once the not defined areas are 0.
  • Not saved areas would show their circle in red.
  • Press the button "New apartment" or press N to create a new apartment.
  • Press back-key to delete the selected apartment.
  • Press the trash icon to delete an apartment.
  • Press 1 to 9 to select the apartment.
  • Hold space to hide the features and see the floorplan.
  • Hold m to hide the already splitted areas.
  • Press Tab to switch to the next unit when typing the Unit Id.
View controls
  • Mousewheel to zoom.
  • Right click and drag to pan.
  • Press 0 to reset zoom and panning.
