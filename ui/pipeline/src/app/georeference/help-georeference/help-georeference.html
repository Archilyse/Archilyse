Controls and Keyboard Shortcuts
Instructions
  • Press the button "Save" to save the changes.
  • Drag and drop the building to move it.
  • Hold any key to enter Rotation Mode, while in Rotation Mode: drag the building to rotate it.
Legend
  • Blue building is the one to georeference
  • Yellow buildings are the ones previously georeferenced
  • Back buildings
View controls
  • Mousewheel to zoom.
  • Left click and drag to pan.
  • Press 0 to reset zoom and panning.
  • Hold key x to see the original floorplan with full screen.
  • Press key y to open the original floorplan in a new window.
